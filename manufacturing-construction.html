MANUFACTURING CONTRACTORS

Because manufacturing projects are notoriously complex, manufacturing contractors must be expertly qualified in the industry to ensure success. Bethels Construction is a leading industrial builder and top manufacturing construction contractor. Utilizing a tried and true design-build approach, Bethels Construction simplifies the complexities of manufacturing construction projects while reducing costs and providing integrative and intelligent solutions.

From large site developments to existing facility renovations and expansions, Bethels Construction challenges the status quo throughout the design and construction processes to achieve the best outcome for clients and their businesses.

FACILITY TYPES

Manufacturing facilities are unique to the products being produced. Understanding how the space will be utilized and the types of equipment and their configurations is crucial to designing and building a functional and cost-effective facility. High hazard specifications, heavy loaded floors/equipment foundations, and clear height are just a few of the elements that must be considered when determining the proper manufacturing construction facility type.

PROJECT TYPES

Manufacturing construction project types include new construction, expansions, and renovations. Location, site selection, existing facility conditions, and future growth of the client’s business are all factors that should be considered when choosing a project type.
