About Bethels Construction

Bethels Construction is construction firm that deals basically in the design and construction of industrial buildings majorly in the area of warehousing. With over 20 years of working and partnership experiences our knowlegeable and reliable team have been performing exceptionally well in carrying out projects globally particularly in the United States. Bethels Construction's unmatched experience, the expertise of our staff, and best-in-class solutions has resulted in thousands of successfully completed projects nationwide and the very best construction process for our customers and associates.

Superior Project Delivery

Bethels Construction's design-build delivery method combines quality of design and construction, management of capital expense, and adherence to schedule into a single point of responsibility. As a result, our customers reap the benefits of total integration. Our team of design-build construction experts effectively gain an understanding of each client’s business and analyze the key components to reduce exposure and provide the very best value.
