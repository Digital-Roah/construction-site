Industrial

Having constructed millions of square feet in industrial projects, Bethels Construction and its team of design/build construction experts take on even the most complex projects regardless of circumstances or scale. From distribution facilities to high hazard environments, Bethels Construction understands the unique challenges clients face. Utilizing a tried and true design/build approach, Bethels Construction simplifies complexities while reducing costs and providing integrative and intelligent solutions for clients spanning all industry types. With a hands on approach that includes a single source of contact and turnkey solutions, Bethels Construction provides clients with peace of mind that their facilities will be completed to their exact specifications. From large site development projects to existing facility expansions, Bethels Construction challenges the status quo throughout the design and construction processes to achieve the best possible outcome for clients and their businesses.
