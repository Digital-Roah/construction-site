Careers

With Best-in-Class Benefits and Superior Compensation, Bethels Construction is not only a Great Place to Work, but the very place to build a Career.

The Best Place to Build a Career

As a leader in the design-build industry, we know that the best work requires the best talent. That’s why we are committed to taking care of our greatest asset – our team. Bethels Construction’s best-in-class benefits and superior compensation is what sets us apart from the competition and continues to attract associates who are the best in the business.

From Project Managers and Superintendents to Architects and countless other roles that help make our business a success, Bethels Construction is dedicated to creating a workplace in which associates at all levels feel valued and empowered to succeed.

Featured Positions

1. PROJECT MANAGERS

Bethels Construction Project Managers take complete ownership of a project and are given the tools to succeed from day one. From the proposal and preconstruction phase, to buyout, construction, and project closeout, Bethels Construction’s design-build process allows for Project Managers to be involved in all stages of a project, from concept to completion. Project Managers travel approximately four days per month and are never required to relocate to a jobsite.

2. SUPERINTENDENTS

Bethels Construction Superintendents are our onsite presence at each of our jobsites nationwide. From the West Coast to the Midwest, East Coast, and everywhere in-between, our Superintendents help bring clients’ visions to life, regardless of project location, complexity, or scale. Superintendents are provided with living and travel reimbursement, allowing them to live anywhere in the country.

3. BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

Bethels Construction Business Development Associates immediately benefit from our established, nationwide presence and best-in-class reputation, resulting in limitless sales opportunity. Our business development associates build and drive new client relationships, expand market opportunities, and ensure our clients receive the best possible experience.

3. ARCHITECTURAL & STRUCTURAL DESIGN

Bethels Construction’s in-house firm, GMA Design Group, is made up of a talented team of architectural & structural design professionals including Architects, Architectural Project Managers, Structural Engineers, and more. Our design associates work with clients and the entire project team from concept through construction and completion, refining the client’s vision down to the finest details.

WORK HARD PLAY HARD CULTURE

The hard work of our team never goes unnoticed, so we make cultivating an upbeat culture and celebrating our success a top priority. From regular happy hours to company-wide incentive trips, Bethels Construction strives to make our workplace one that is a fun and enjoyable environment for our associates.

Bethels Construction’S COMMITMENT TO DIVERSITY & INCLUSION

Bethels Construction is committed to identifying and implementing ways to advance diversity, equity, and inclusion in the construction industry. Beyond standard corporate policies and practices, Bethels Construction extends our commitment to creating an inclusive culture to our recruiting, training, and subcontractor selection. We know that the best work requires the best people and that the diversity of all those involved in the construction process enhances our ability to deliver the very best solutions to our customers.
